Equity and Equality

Equity

"Equity" means giving everyone what they need to be successful,
even if it's not the same for everyone.


Equality

"Equality" means treating everyone the same way, regardless of their
individual needs or circumstances.


Imagine a school that practices equality. It provides the same textbooks, facilities,
and teachers to all students. However, some students may have learning disabilities or come from disadvantaged backgrounds
, making it difficult for them to succeed with only equal resources. On the other hand,
a school that practices equity would provide additional resources such as tutoring or individualized support to students who need it,
ensuring that all students have an equal opportunity to thrive academically.
